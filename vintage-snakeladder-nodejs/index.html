📋 Game Rules

Objective

Be the first player to reach square 100!

How to Play

  • Players take turns rolling the dice
  • Move your piece forward by the number shown on the dice
  • If you land on a ladder bottom, climb up!
  • If you land on a snake head, slide down!
  • You must roll the exact number to reach square 100

Special Rules

  • If you roll more than needed to reach 100, you bounce back
  • Landing on the same square as another player sends them back to start
